Message Of The Day

  • Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) coming to Brock

    Faculty, staff and students are encouraged to enroll in MFA now by following the instructions on the Information Technology Services Website. MFA will be required to login into select Brock University applications later this year. Enrolling now will make for a smoother transition to MFA once enforced across campus.

    Sakai Deletion - Winter 2015 + Spring/Summer 2016

    Beginning June 26th, 2020, Isaak-Sakai course sites that are 4 years or older will be deleted. This includes courses from Winter 2015 and Spring/Summer 2016. You would have received an email if you are listed as the primary contact for a course site within Isaak-Sakai that is queued for deletion.

    If you’re unsure which of your Isaak-Sakai course sites are queued for deletion, visit https://kumu.brocku.ca/sakai/Isaak-Sakai_course_sites_to_be_deleted for steps on how locate them.

    Older Isaak-Sakai site content can be copied into newer sites or exported from key tools. Steps on how to export content have been documented at https://kumu.brocku.ca/sakai/How_do_I_export_site_content

    Should you wish to retain site content, the Centre for Pedagogical Innovation (CPI) is available to help instructors retrieve, export and/or copy site data. Please email edtech@brocku.ca if you have any questions about the process.

    More information about the scheduled deletion of courses can be found at: https://brocku.ca/pedagogical-innovation/teaching-tools/issak-sakai/
